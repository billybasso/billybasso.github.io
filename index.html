Animal Well is a videogame about secrets. Find your way through a surrealist labyrinth, marooned in time and space, where everything should be questioned. It takes inspiration from the survival horror genre, old PC adventure games, and Nintendo classics such as original Zelda and Metroid.

  • Explore a dense atmospheric puzzle box world with multiple layers of secrets to uncover
  • Completely custom engine/rendering build from scratch -- Animal Well looks and plays like no other game
  • Encounter creatures and sights both beautiful and demented
  • Collect items that offer safety and allow you to manipulate the environment in surprising and meaningful ways


Estimated release date: 2022
Platforms: Not yet announced

Animal Well is a handcrafted experience, with attention payed to every detail. Great care has been taken to ensure It will be enjoyable and giving back far into the future. When you aquire a copy, you're aquiring an artifact with dormant secrets, that may remain hidden forever.

Animal Well has been being solo developed by Billy Basso since late 2017. Billy Basso is a programmer in Chicago who has been making games for the past 10 years. He currently works full time at Level Ex. Prior to that, he was at Netherrealm Studios, and Phosphor Games.
